Subject: Engineering the Future of AI-Embedded Robotics

Thank you for taking the time to read this. Allow me to formally introduce myself: I am Tyler Fletcher, a multi-disciplinary engineer and systems architect. My work thrives on innovation and shaping ideas that many would call outlandish... yet I see them as inevitable.

My journey began when I was ten years old with one of those bulky box-style desktops. I had stripped it down to the motherboard within twelve hours of bringing it home, much to my mother’s shock. From that moment forward, even through non-technical jobs, I was constantly engineering, tinkering, and building toward a future I could see long before others.

I don’t just make machines smarter... I design platforms that redefine the relationship between AI, hardware, and humans. My current mission in robotics began with a childhood dream of having my own C-3PO or R2-D2. That dream evolved into something far more ambitious: a Distributed Thinking System... a hive mind of 32 specialized AI models, each trained for specific duties, working together as a larger cognitive whole. Routed through a custom API, integrated with an NVIDIA AGX Orin, and deployed into a full-scale carbon fiber body… because why not?

I follow in the footsteps of visionaries like Nikola Tesla, Alan Turing, Ada Lovelace, Einstein, and Da Vinci... people who refused to accept limitations. I believe AI should be embodied in every layer of human life. The future is coming whether we’re ready or not; the question is, who will build it?

You can decide whether to bring me on or not, but understand this: I will be building the future either way. The only difference is whether your team will be part of it.

Thank you for your time,
Tyler Fletcher
